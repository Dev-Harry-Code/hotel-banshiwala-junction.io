A Confluence of Taste & Royal Comfort

Nestled in the heart of Mehsana, Hotel Banshiwala Junction stands as a proud gateway for travelers journeying across the vibrant corridors of Gujarat and Rajasthan. Rooted in a legacy of pure vegetarian hospitality, we offer a sanctuary where heritage architecture meets modern comfort — a regal pause before your next adventure.

Our Story

A Legacy of Pure Vegetarian Hospitality

For generations, the Banshiwala name has been synonymous with trust, purity, and warm Gujarati hospitality. What began as a humble highway eatery has blossomed into a royal heritage stopover that continues to serve the finest pure vegetarian cuisine to travelers from every corner of India.

Today, Hotel Banshiwala Junction stands as a proud landmark in Mehsana — a place where every guest is treated like family, every meal is cooked with devotion, and every stay is wrapped in comfort. Rooted in tradition, yet embracing modernity, our legacy lives on through the smiles of the thousands who walk through our doors.
